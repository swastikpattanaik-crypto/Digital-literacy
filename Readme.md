## About This Project

This repository is my Digital Literacy Portfolio built for CSE0001 at
VIT Bhopal University. The project is based on a real-world scenario
where I took on the role of a Student Digital Ambassador — someone who
helps their peers navigate the digital world safely and professionally.

The project has five tasks, each covering one module of the course.
Everything — screenshots, files, checklists, and the full project report
— is organised in this repository.

---

## Task 1 — Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

**Tool Used:** Canva | **Folder:** `task-1-presentation/`

I designed a one-page infographic using Canva aimed at first-year B.Tech
students. It covers four topics — what digital literacy means, useful
digital tools for students (GitHub, Google Docs, LinkedIn), safe internet
practices (strong passwords, 2FA, avoiding unknown links), and how to
maintain a professional online presence.

The goal was to communicate all of this visually on a single page so
any student could understand it at a glance.

## Task 3 — Coding and Collaboration Platforms

**Tools:** HackerRank, Google Forms | **Folder:** `task-3-platforms/`

**Part A — HackerRank:**
I created an account on HackerRank and completed the "Solve Me First"
challenge under the Warmup section. It introduced me to how online
judges work — submitting code and getting it tested automatically —
which is the same format used in placement tests in third and fourth year.

**Part B — Google Forms Quiz:**
I built a five-question Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz on Google Forms,
covering topics like safe internet practices, what digital literacy means,
and how to report cybercrime in India. The form is linked to a Google
Sheet so all responses are recorded automatically.

## Task 4 — Professional Email and Etiquette Guide

**Folder:** `task-4-email-etiquette/`

**Part A — Two Professional Emails:**
I wrote two emails following proper professional email structure —
clear subject line, polite greeting, well-organised body, and a
correct sign-off.

- **Email 1** — A student requesting a three-day assignment extension
  from their professor due to illness.
- **Email 2** — A student expressing interest in a summer internship
  opportunity at a company.

Both drafts are saved in `email 1 and 2` inside the folder above.

**Part B — Social Media Checklist:**
I created a Do's and Don'ts checklist for responsible social media use
as a college student — covering things like protecting personal
information, thinking before posting, and reporting harmful content.
Saved as `checklist.md`.

## Task 5 — Cybercrime Awareness: Phishing

**Folder:** `task-5-cybercrime/`

**Part A — Case Study (`casestudy.md`):**
I wrote a detailed case study on phishing — one of the most common
cybercrimes targeting college students in India. It explains what
phishing is, how an attack unfolds step by step, who is usually
targeted, and what the consequences are for the victim.

**Part B — Prevention Checklist (`checklist.md`):**
I created a "Stay Safe Online" checklist with 10 actionable tips for
college students, including two tips specifically about UPI and financial
safety. The checklist also includes the official reporting channels:

- 🌐 National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- 📞 Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24x7)
