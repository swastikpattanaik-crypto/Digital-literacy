# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students
## Created by: Swastik Pattanaik | CSE | VIT Bhopal

---

## ✅ DO's

1. **Do** keep your LinkedIn profile updated with your education,
   skills, and projects.
2. **Do** use your college email for professional platform sign-ups.
3. **Do** think before you post — ask yourself if you would be
   comfortable with your professor or future employer seeing it.
4. **Do** follow and engage with industry professionals,
   professors, and peers on LinkedIn.
5. **Do** report any cyberbullying, harassment, or suspicious
   activity to the platform immediately.

---

## ❌ DON'Ts

1. **Don't** share your personal information (phone number,
   address, Aadhaar, UPI PIN) publicly on any platform.
2. **Don't** post or share content that is offensive, abusive,
   or discriminatory in any form.
3. **Don't** engage in arguments or post negative comments about
   your college, professors, or classmates online.
4. **Don't** accept friend or connection requests from people
   you do not know personally.
5. **Don't** use social media to share exam papers, internal
   marks, or any confidential college information.
